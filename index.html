Official Privacy Policy for Google Play Applications

Last updated: 2 January 2026

Overview

Passive Investments Learning ("we", "our", or "the Developer") develops and publishes casual mobile applications available on Google Play.

User privacy is a fundamental principle for us. Our casual apps are designed to function without collecting, storing, processing, or sharing any personal or sensitive user data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any form of personal information or device-related data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Location information
  • Usage statistics or behavioral data
  • Financial or payment information

Our applications do not communicate with external servers and do not transmit user data of any kind.

2. User Accounts

Our casual apps do not require user registration, account creation, or login.

All app functionality is accessible without providing personal information.

3. Third-Party SDKs and Services

Passive Investments Learning applications do not include third-party SDKs or services, including:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Attribution services
  • Crash reporting SDKs

As a result, no user data is shared with any third party.

4. Google Play Data Safety Compliance

In accordance with Google Play’s Data Safety requirements, we confirm that:

  • No user data is collected
  • No user data is shared
  • No tracking technologies are used
  • No data is stored on external servers

This Privacy Policy accurately reflects the disclosures provided in the Google Play Console.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our casual applications are suitable for a general audience, including children.

Since no personal data is collected, we do not knowingly collect, store, or process information from children under the age of 13 or from any other users.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or app functionality.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
